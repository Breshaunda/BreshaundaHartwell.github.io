How Spirituality Can Relieve Stress

"Research shows that spirituality can benefit both the mind and the body. Whether someone is coping with cancer or clinical depression, spiritual exercises help them increase acceptance, decrease negative emotions, find meaning, and deepen their relationships with others. Understanding Spirituality

September 25th, 2023 9am CST

For a long time, religious practices were linked to mental illness. Luminaries in the field of psychology, such as Freud and Charcot, envisioned religion as a pathology that indicated mental health problems in patients. For this reason, psychology avoided religion—and its less-formalized cousin, spirituality—for many decades. Today, we see this viewpoint as counter to the human condition. Spirituality is a widespread (nearly universal) human trait that people approach in many different ways but with the same goals. They wish to find meaning and value in everyday life, to make sense of life, and to connect themselves with something larger. Spirituality is a way of integrating our own experiences with the idea of something more overarching and more timeless than ourselves.
